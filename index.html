Title

TAMARA AND THE GHOST

Author

PHILOMINA CHINATU AMADI(MRS)

Description

The book Tamara And The Ghost, teaches morals lessons that reaffirms discipline as a great virtue that must be enforced in our homes, schools and society at large. The girl Tamara, in the book is disobedient and lacks discipline. Tamara’s parents were afraid to carryout a disciplinary action on her each time she disobeys them, because she is their only child. A rebuke does not kill. Inspite of being warned by her parents never to leave the house on that Eke market day. Tamara left their compound and went to pick a golden apple at the square. Every act of disobedience and indiscipline has its own punishment and unforgettable pain.

Meet Our Author
